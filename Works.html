Works

  • Tamil writer - He was bestowed the title "Bharathi" for hs excellence in poetry.
  • Poet - He was a pioneer of modern Tamil poetry and is considered one of the greatest Tamil literary figures of all time.
  • Jouralist - He worked as a journalist with many newspapers, including The Hindu, Bala Bharata, Vijaya, Chakravarhini, The Swadesamitran and India
  • Indian independence activist - As a independence activist he wrote fiery songs kindling patriotism during the Indian independence movement.
  • Social reformer - He fought for the emancipation of women, against child marriage, vehemently opposed the caste system, and stood for reforming society and religion
  • Polyglot - He knew 32 languages including 3 foreign languages
